Welcome to the DevConf Example blog. This is a sample post shipped with
`ddd-core` to give the blog index something to render in standalone builds.

The blog feature in core supports:

- Multiple authors per post (referenced from `authors.yml`)
- Standard markdown + MDX components
- An RSS feed at `/blog.rss.xml`
- Per-post sharing metadata derived from the `title` and post body

## Why have a blog?

Most community conferences end up with announcements that don't quite fit
anywhere else — chairperson reports, sponsor highlights, AGM notes, "save the
date" posts, reflections after the event. The blog is the catch-all for these.

It's also a nice low-friction way for organising committee members to write up
their thoughts publicly, which helps demystify how the conference runs.

## Replacing this post

If you're forking from `ddd-core`, delete this file and write your own. The
authors file at `content/blog/authors.yml` is also generic — replace it with
your real committee members.
