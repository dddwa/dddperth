This is a placeholder for a Country/land acknowledgement. Conferences held in
regions with Country acknowledgement conventions (Australia, Aotearoa, parts of
North America) should rewrite this with a meaningful acknowledgement specific to
the lands their conference is held on, and then set `homepage.acknowledgementSlug`
in `conference/manifest.ts` to `'_acknowledgement'`.

DDD Perth's manifest sets this slug; the stub's doesn't. Compare the two to see
the opt-in pattern.
