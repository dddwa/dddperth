Sponsorship makes community-run tech conferences possible. DevConf Example offers
several sponsorship tiers, each with its own benefits and visibility on this site.

> This page is shipped as a generic template. Forks should rewrite with their own
> tier pricing, benefits, and call-to-action.

## Why sponsor?

- Reach a focused audience of practising software professionals
- Support an inclusive event that prioritises accessibility and diversity
- Connect with potential hires in a community setting

## Tiers

### Platinum

The lead-partner tier. Limited to one or two sponsors per year. Includes:

- Premier logo placement on the home page and all year pages
- A full booth space at the venue
- A pre-conference quote published on the sponsorship page
- Speaking slot consideration (subject to CFP review)
- 10 conference tickets

### Gold

The most common premium tier. Includes:

- Logo on home page and year pages
- Standard booth space
- 5 conference tickets

### Digital

Logo-only sponsorship — no booth, no tickets. Ideal for organisations that want to
demonstrate community support without on-site activation.

### Community

For non-profits, student groups, meetup communities, and other organisations whose
work aligns with the conference's purpose. No fee — we exchange visibility for
community partnership.
