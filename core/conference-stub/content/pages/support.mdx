If you need help on conference day, here's where to look.

## At the venue

- **Registration desk** — first port of call for anything: lost property, accessibility,
  general questions
- **Code of Conduct leads** — wear high-vis vests; available at registration all day
- **First aid** — kit and trained contact at the registration desk
- **Volunteer floor walkers** — wear coloured shirts; can answer most logistics questions

## Common questions

### I can't find my session room

The mobile app (or this site) has a venue map under each session. Look for the
room number on the back of your badge — it shows your selected sessions.

### My talk got cancelled / room changed

We announce changes on the conference Slack workspace (link emailed to ticket
holders the night before) and via the mobile app. Floor walkers can point you to
the new location.

### I'm having trouble with the wifi

Wifi credentials are on your badge and on signs in every room. If those don't
work, the registration desk can reset your access.

### I lost my badge

Come to the registration desk with photo ID and we'll print a replacement.

### I want to leave early

That's fine — just please drop your badge in a bin near the exit so we don't
list you as missing.
