## What we collect

When you interact with this site or buy a ticket, we may collect:

- Your name and email (when you register, buy a ticket, or submit a CFP entry)
- Your dietary requirements and accessibility needs (when you buy a ticket)
- Anonymous voting choices (when you participate in talk voting)
- Standard web request logs (IP address, browser, requested page) for at most 30 days

## How we use it

- To send you ticket and event-day information
- To plan catering and accessibility
- To shape the agenda based on community voting
- To improve the site (aggregated, never tied to individuals)

## Who we share it with

We share data with:

- **Tito** — our ticketing platform (only ticket purchase information)
- **Sessionize** — our CFP and agenda platform (only CFP submissions)
- **Cloudflare** — our hosting provider (only request logs)

We do not sell or share your data with sponsors, advertisers, or anyone else.

## Your rights

You can request:

- A copy of the data we hold about you
- Correction of any inaccurate data
- Deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)

Email [info@example.test](mailto:info@example.test) to make a request.

## Cookies

This site uses cookies only for essential functionality (session, theme preference).
We do not use analytics or advertising cookies.
