Tickets for DevConf Example 2025 are available through Tito at our (fictional)
ticketing page. Pricing follows three tiers timed across the year:

| Release | Window | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Early Bird | April – June | $50 |
| General | July – early October | $80 |
| Last Minute | The week before the conference | $120 |

## What does a ticket include?

- Full conference day access (all four tracks)
- Lunch and snacks throughout the day
- Welcome reception the evening before
- Access to the conference Slack workspace for one month after the event
- A swag bag with sponsor goodies

## Refunds and transfers

Tickets are refundable up to two weeks before the conference. You can transfer your
ticket to someone else up until the day before — log into Tito and update the
attendee details.

## Need a financial assistance ticket?

If the ticket price is a barrier to attending, email
[info@example.test](mailto:info@example.test) and we'll work something out. We
keep a small number of complimentary tickets each year specifically for this
purpose.

## Group bookings

Groups of 5 or more get a 10% discount. Email
[info@example.test](mailto:info@example.test) before booking.
