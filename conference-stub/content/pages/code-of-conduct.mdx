DevConf Example is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience
for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual
orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion
(or lack thereof), or technology choices. We do not tolerate harassment of conference
participants in any form.

This is a sample code of conduct shipped with `ddd-core` for forks to start from.
**Forks should replace this content** with a code of conduct reviewed by their own
organising committee and legal advisors. A good starting point is the
[Conference Code of Conduct](https://confcodeofconduct.com/) or the
[Berlin Code of Conduct](https://berlincodeofconduct.org/).

## Expected behaviour

- Be welcoming, friendly, and patient
- Be considerate and respectful
- Be careful with the words you choose
- Seek to understand other points of view
- Listen actively

## Unacceptable behaviour

Unacceptable behaviour includes (but is not limited to):

- Harassment of any kind, including unwelcome sexual attention
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Sustained disruption of talks or other events
- Inappropriate physical contact
- Photography or recording of attendees without their consent

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this code of conduct, please
report it immediately to a member of the organising team. Look for the high-vis
vests at the venue, find a Code of Conduct lead at the registration desk, or
email [conduct@example.test](mailto:conduct@example.test).

All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Consequences

Anyone asked to stop unacceptable behaviour is expected to comply immediately. The
organising team may take any action they deem appropriate, up to and including
expulsion from the conference without refund.
