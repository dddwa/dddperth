DevConf Example is an in-person event, and we want everyone to leave healthier
than they arrived (or at least no worse).

## If you're unwell

**Please don't come.** Tickets are refundable up to two weeks before the conference,
and same-day or next-day transfers are allowed if you can find someone to take
your spot. Email [info@example.test](mailto:info@example.test).

## Hand sanitiser

Stations are placed at all entrances, near catering, and outside restrooms.

## Masks

Masking is welcomed but not required. The organising committee may update this
policy if local public health guidance changes ahead of the conference.

## Air quality

The venue circulates outside air and has HEPA filtration in the main hall.

## First aid

A first-aid kit and a designated medical contact are available at the registration
desk throughout the day. For anything urgent, call emergency services first, then
notify the registration desk.

## Quiet room

A quiet room is available for anyone who needs a break from the noise and crowds.
Signposted from the main hall. No conversations, no laptops with sound, no phone
calls.
