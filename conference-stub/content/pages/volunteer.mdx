DevConf Example needs around 40 volunteers each year to keep the conference running
smoothly. If you've ever thought "I'd love to help out" — this is your sign.

## What volunteers do

- **Registration** (early morning shift): check attendees in, hand out badges
- **Room hosting**: introduce speakers, manage Q&A, run errands
- **Sponsor coordination**: help sponsors set up booths, point attendees their way
- **Catering**: direct people to their lunch zone, restock coffee
- **Floor walkers**: answer questions, give directions
- **AV runners**: hold mics for Q&A, troubleshoot speaker setup
- **Photography**: candid shots throughout the day (photographers welcome to bring own gear)

## What you get

- A volunteer T-shirt and lunch
- Conference attendance — you'll catch most talks in your assigned room
- A volunteer thank-you drink at the after-party
- A genuinely good time and a closer look at how the event runs

## How to sign up

> The volunteer signup form for this stub is **not wired up** — `volunteerForm` is
> set to `undefined` in the years-index. To enable volunteer signups in a fork:
>
> 1. Set up a Tito event for volunteer registration, or a Salesmate form
> 2. Wire it into `conference/config/years-index.ts` `volunteerForm` field
> 3. The signup link will appear on this page automatically

## What we expect from volunteers

- Arrival at the volunteer briefing (usually 60 minutes before doors open)
- Reliability — if you sign up for a shift, please commit
- A friendly attitude — you're the face of the conference for many attendees
- Adherence to the [code of conduct](/code-of-conduct)
