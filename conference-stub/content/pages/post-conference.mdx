DevConf Example 2025 is a wrap. Thanks to everyone who attended, spoke, sponsored,
or volunteered.

## Recordings

Main-stage recordings are typically posted to YouTube within 2-4 weeks of the
conference. We'll update this page with links as they go live.

## Slide decks

We collect slide decks from speakers and link to them from the agenda page next
to each session. If you spoke and haven't sent yours yet, email
[info@example.test](mailto:info@example.test).

## Photos

A photo gallery from the day is posted to our Flickr account once the official
photographer's edit is ready.

## Feedback

The feedback form is open for **one week** after the conference. Five minutes of
your time helps us plan next year — even (especially) the constructive criticism.

## What's next?

- **Next year**: planning kicks off in February. Sponsorship enquiries open early
  March.
- **CFP for next year**: typically opens in June.
- **Newsletter**: subscribe via the link in the footer to get one (1) email when
  ticket sales open.

## A community thank-you

DevConf Example is run entirely by volunteers, supported by sponsors who believe
in community-led tech events. If either of those things is something you'd like to
help with, get in touch — we always need more hands and we always need more
sponsors.
