A guide to navigating DevConf Example on the day. Bookmark this page on your phone
— it's also surfaced inside the mobile app (when a fork has one configured).

## Schedule overview

| Time | What |
|---|---|
| 08:00 | Registration opens, coffee available |
| 09:00 | Opening keynote |
| 09:45 | Morning sessions begin (4 parallel tracks) |
| 12:30 | Lunch (allocated zones — check your badge) |
| 14:00 | Afternoon sessions begin |
| 17:00 | Closing keynote |
| 17:45 | Sponsor showcase + drinks |
| 19:00 | Day ends; speakers dinner for invited guests |

## Lunch

Lunch is served in four zones around the venue. Your badge has a zone letter on it
(A/B/C/D) — that's where you eat. This keeps queues manageable when 1,000+ people
are all hungry at once.

Three rotating meal options per zone, with vegetarian, gluten-free, and dairy-free
labels.

## Sessions

Sessions are 30 or 45 minutes. Most rooms have a 10-minute changeover. Use the
mobile app (or this site) to plan your day in advance.

## Sponsor area

Sponsors set up booths in the main hall. Stop by — many run prize draws, demo
their products, or are actively hiring. Sponsor support is what keeps tickets
affordable, so a quick "thanks for being here" goes a long way.

## Getting help

- **Lost**: signs are everywhere, but volunteers in coloured shirts can direct you
- **Accessibility**: speak to anyone at the registration desk
- **Code of conduct concerns**: a Code of Conduct lead is at registration all day
- **Lost property**: handed in at the registration desk

## After the conference

A post-conference page goes live the morning after with:

- Links to recorded talks (typically 2-4 weeks later)
- Speaker slide decks (shared as authors send them in)
- Photos from the event
- Feedback form (open for one week)
